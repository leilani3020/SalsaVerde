Why Network?

Travel has become quite common. We visit different cities for different reasons. Salsa dancers may visit a city to see family or for work but while we are there we most likely wonder what the Salsa scene is like and if there is a place for us to dance during our visit.

My Traveling Salsa Experience.

I have learned that it is difficult to navigate the Salsa scene unless I already know somebody who can tell me where to go. Even the internet can have outdated information regarding places to dance or even none at all. So, my hope is to build a network of Salsa dance information for dancers who travel and don't want to start all over in each new city trying to learn their way around the Salsa scene.
